Hi, I'm Ruotong.

I recently graduated from Macalester College, where I studied Sociology, Mathematics and Computer Science. I am currently a research assistant under Prof. Haiyi Zhu in the Human-Computer Interaction Institute (HCII) at Carnegie Mellon University.

I do research in the field of human-computer Interaction and social computing. My current project seeks to identify and correct gender bias in Wikipedia content.
